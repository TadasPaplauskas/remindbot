Remindbot

Hi, remindbot will remind you about stuff.

What is this?
It's a simple messenger bot that does one think and it does it very well: it reminds you about stuff.

What's wrong with regular calendar apps?
Nothing. I still use google calendar and trello boards to get work done. However, don't you sometimes just wish that you could ask someone to remind you to buy some milk, take out the trash or call your mom? This bot is designed exactly for that - setting reminders for small tasks on the go.
